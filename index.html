About us

Sugna Desert Safari is a dedicated tour operator based in Rajasthan, especially for Jaisalmer. It offers a variety of tour packages, tourist pots, and mystery places that no other traveling provider can offer. It also offers luxury Swiss Tent camping with incredible service and great facilities. It is a perfect place for an off-beat holiday with basic hygiene and good food. The Camp offers packages for families to honeymooners, from corporate holidays to individual, and honeymoon trips to students for photography or research.

Jaisalmer has a big palette and innovative ideas to make each and every holiday customized. Each hour of the holiday should be like a celebration of desert life, be it a camel safari, meeting villagers, dune bashing, or archery. Sugna Desert Safari Jaisalmer offers enthralling packages at great prices. They will tell you how to get the best of the traditional culture and rituals that are being followed at Jaisalmer. Sugna Desert Safari will continue to provide the best of service and great facilities, so give them a call today and explore some of our enthralling packages at great prices. The camp is located in Kanoi Village, near Jaisalmer, in a peaceful location surrounded by sand dunes. It offers luxury tented accommodations, camel safaris, and unforgettable adventures in the golden sand dunes. Guests can enjoy a feeling of seclusion and escapism from the outside world.
