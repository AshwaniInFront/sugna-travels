Maharaja Package

It is known for its traditional mud-based architecture, which has been used to construct cottages. Mud has been used in various ways to construct cottages, from building walls to creating roofs. The use of mud also helps keep temperatures cool during hot summers and warm during cold winters, making it an ideal location for those seeking a peaceful retreat away from the hustle and bustle of urban life. Inspired by the architecture of Jaisalmer, erected on Yellowstone, these comfortable cottages are so cozy that you will feel like a home away from home, perfect for couples and families. Our cottage rooms are so cozy that you will feel like a home away from home.
